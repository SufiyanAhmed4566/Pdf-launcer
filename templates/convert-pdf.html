Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about converting PDF files

What formats can I convert PDF files to?

You can convert PDF files to Word documents (.docx), plain text (.txt), and extract images. You can also convert images to PDF format.

Will the formatting be preserved when converting PDF to Word?

Our conversion algorithm does its best to preserve formatting, including fonts, images, and layout. However, complex layouts may require minor adjustments in Word.

Are my files secure during conversion?

Yes, absolutely! Your files are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to any server. We cannot access, view, or store your documents.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Currently, our tool processes one file at a time for conversion. However, you can quickly process multiple files one after another.

What image formats are supported for PDF conversion?

We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, and other common image formats for conversion to PDF. You can convert multiple images into a single PDF document.
