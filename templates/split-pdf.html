Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about splitting PDF files

How many ways can I split a PDF?

You can split PDFs in multiple ways: by page range (split at specific page), extract single pages, or create multiple splits from one document.

Is there any file size limit?

No, there are no file size limitations. You can split PDF files of any size. The processing happens directly in your browser for maximum security.

Are my files secure during splitting?

Yes, absolutely! Your files are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to any server. We cannot access, view, or store your documents.

Can I split multiple PDFs at once?

Currently, our tool processes one PDF file at a time for splitting. However, you can quickly process multiple files one after another.

What happens to the split PDF quality?

The split PDFs maintain the original quality of the input file. We don't compress or reduce quality during the splitting process.
