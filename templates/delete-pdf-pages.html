Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about deleting PDF pages

How many pages can I delete at once?

You can delete as many pages as you want, but you must leave at least one page in the document. You cannot delete all pages from a PDF file.

Can I delete page ranges?

Yes, you can delete individual pages or entire page ranges. Use the range input to quickly select multiple consecutive pages for deletion.

Are my files secure during deletion?

Yes, absolutely! Your files are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to any server. We cannot access, view, or store your documents.

What happens to the remaining PDF quality?

The remaining PDF maintains the original quality. We don't compress or reduce quality during the page deletion process.

Can I preview the pages before deletion?

Yes, you can see all pages visually and select which ones to delete. The interface shows you exactly which pages will be removed.
