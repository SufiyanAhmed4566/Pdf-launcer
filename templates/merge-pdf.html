Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about merging PDF files

How many PDF files can I merge at once?

You can merge unlimited PDF files at once. There are no restrictions on the number of files or pages you can combine using our PDF merger tool.

Is there any file size limit?

No, there are no file size limitations. You can merge PDF files of any size. The processing happens directly in your browser for maximum security and performance.

Are my files secure?

Yes, absolutely! Your files are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to any server. We cannot access, view, or store your documents.

What happens to the merged PDF quality?

The merged PDF maintains the original quality of all input files. We don't compress or reduce quality during the merging process.

Can I reorder the PDF files before merging?

Yes, you can easily reorder the files by dragging and dropping them in your preferred sequence before merging.
